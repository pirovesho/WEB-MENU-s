Staying at Illyria Hotel

Every stay is a journey into elegance. Enjoy spacious rooms with stunning views, refined amenities, and a warm atmosphere that feels like home. Let comfort and sophistication blend perfectly during your visit to Shkodër.

1 queen bed

Our exclusive Lake View Double Room features one queen bed and opens to breathtaking views of Shkodër’s shimmering lake and legendary castle. As the only room of this type, it offers a rare sense of privacy and intimacy, blending refined Illyrian-inspired design with calming tones and thoughtfully curated details. Every moment here invites deep relaxation, comfort, and a truly unforgettable stay—ideal for guests seeking something unique, elegant, and soulful.
